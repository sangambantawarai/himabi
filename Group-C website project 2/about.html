School Introduction

Himalaya Secondary School (established in 2012 BS.) located at Damak-2, Jhapa, Koshi Province, is a community school in eastern part of Nepal at Damak, Jhapa is run by the devoted, dedicated, as well as committed administrators and educationalist with the objective of promoting quality education by maintaining good pedagogical environment through modern, scientific, psychological teaching method. The school has done its best to improve the quality of its studies. This is proved by its 100% SEE and +2 results in the successive years. The systemic guidance and strict observation to the individual students is proved to be fruitful to improve the base of the students maintaining its motto “Discipline and Quality.” Since 2062 BS, the school has started (10 + 2) classes in Management, Education, Humanities, Science and Computer Engineering stream affiliated to National Education Board, Nepal. In the course of time, Himalaya Secondary School has successfully developed adequate infrastructure required for materializing its aims and objectives by its own efforts, inclusive and sincere co- operation of parents and well-wishers. The school has envisaged from its very inception to serve the nation by producing mentally and physically versatile citizens capable of coping with the challenges of 21st century so that they can do their best for nation, being fully responsible, conscious, dedicated, self confident and energetic personage.
